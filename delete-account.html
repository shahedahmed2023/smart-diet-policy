Delete Your Account

To delete your Smart Diet account:

  1. Open the app
  2. Log in if you are not already signed in
  3. Go to the Profile tab
  4. Scroll down to the bottom of the page
  5. Tap Delete Account
  6. Confirm your action when prompted

What happens when you delete your account

When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed, including:

  • Profile information
  • Health and diet data
  • Authentication account

No data is retained after deletion.
